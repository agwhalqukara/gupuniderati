Beyonc Drunk in Love Lyrics

August 2024 · 4 minute read

“Drunk in Love Lyrics” is a popular song by American singer Beyoncé. It was released in December 2013 as the lead single from her self-titled fifth studio album, “Beyoncé.” The song features guest vocals from Beyoncé’s husband, rapper Jay-Z.

About

Beyoncé

Beyoncé, whose full name is Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter, is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman. She was born on September 4, 1981, in Houston, Texas. Beyoncé rose to fame in the late 1990s as the lead vocalist of the R&B girl group Destiny’s Child.

After achieving success with Destiny’s Child, Beyoncé embarked on a solo career in 2003 with the release of her debut album, “Dangerously in Love.” The album spawned several hit singles, including “Crazy in Love” featuring Jay-Z, and established Beyoncé as a solo artist. Since then, she has released multiple highly successful albums, including “B’Day” (2006), “I Am… Sasha Fierce” (2008), “4” (2011), “Beyoncé” (2013), “Lemonade” (2016), and “Everything Is Love” (2018) in collaboration with Jay-Z as The Carters.

As a performer, Beyoncé is known for her powerful vocals, dynamic stage presence, and elaborate live performances. She has received numerous accolades throughout her career, including Grammy Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and MTV Video Music Awards. In addition to her music, Beyoncé has also ventured into acting, appearing in films such as “Dreamgirls” (2006), “The Pink Panther” (2006), and “Cadillac Records” (2008).

Beyoncé is recognized not only for her artistic contributions but also for her activism and philanthropy. She has been involved in various initiatives supporting social justice, women’s empowerment, and disaster relief efforts.
