Johnny Depp, Amber Heard Kiss at TIFF 2015: Photos

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Hello, smooches! An affectionate Amber Heard and her husband, Johnny Depp, packed on the PDA at the Toronto International Film Festival on Monday, Sept. 14.

The couple were snapped exchanging an intimate kiss on the lips as they walked the red carpet together for the world premiere of his new movie, Black Mass.

Heard, 29, looked gorgeous in a black dress with a sweetheart neckline from Victoria Beckham’s Resort 2016 collection. She paired her outfit with red satin pumps and a swipe of matte cherry lip color, which matched her husband’s button-down shirt.

Depp, 52, pulled together a three-piece gray suit for his film premiere, and accessorized his outfit with layered necklaces.

While Monday night was all about Depp — who is getting serious Oscar buzz for his role as Boston mobster James “Whitey” Bulger in the flick — the actor supported his wife the previous evening at the premiere of her new movie, The Danish Girl. Prior to that, the pair hit the Venice Film Festival in Italy on Sept. 5, for another joint red-carpet appearance.

Depp and Heard met on the set of their film, The Rum Diary, in 2009. They secretly tied the knot this past February and held a beachfront ceremony in the Bahamas for their close family and friends. Prior to Heard, Depp was in a 14-year relationship with actress Vanessa Paradis, with whom he shares two kids, Lily-Rose and Jack.
