Who Is Ana Capeta?

Ana Capeta is a skilled striker who currently plays for Sporting CP in the top women’s football league in Portugal, known as the Campeonato Nacional Feminino.

Additionally, she represents the Portugal women’s national team on the international stage.

Throughout her career, Ana Capeta has achieved success with Sporting Clube de Portugal and earned caps for the Portugal national team.

She showcased her skills during the 2019 FIFA Women’s World Cup qualifying cycle.

Ana Capeta Career

She started her career as a youth player for various clubs in her hometown of Beja, before joining Atlético Ouriense in 2013.

Ana then moved to CAC in 2015, but did not make any appearances for the team.

She signed for Sporting CP in 2016 and became one of the most prolific scorers in the Portuguese league.

Capeta won the league title, the cup, and the super cup with Sporting CP in 2017/2018.

She also played for PSV and Famalicão on loan in 2021 and 2022, respectively.

She has since scored six goals in 23 matches.
