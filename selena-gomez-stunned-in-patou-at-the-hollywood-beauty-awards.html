Selena Gomez Stunned In Patou At The Hollywood Beauty Awards

Selena Gomez stunned in Patou at the Hollywood Beauty Awards and fans are taking notice of the look. Styled by Kate Young, Selena wore a pale pink, off-shoulder dress. The dress isn’t available for sale but is getting a mixed response from Selena’s fans. Some love the look on Selena and many said the pale pink color was a perfect complement for Selena’s natural skin tone. On the other hand, some have said they aren’t a fan of the off-shoulder look and the long-dropped cape-like sleeves.

Kate Young is known for creating unique, stylish looks for Selena and she didn’t disappoint with this outfit. Selena paired the pale-pink Patou dress with a pair of silver, strappy heels from Aquazzura. The shoes retail for approximately $950 and are in demand.

For jewelry, Selena opted for Roberto Coin and wore their Venetian ring, which retails for approximately $3,000 and Roberto Coin diamond hoop earrings that go for approximately $3,100.

Selena’s hair and makeup looked perfect and Marissa Marina created the look. Selena wore her hair up high bun with loose tendrils that framed her face. Hung Vango did Selena’s makeup that featured deep, earthy tones on her eyes and a medium-red tone for her lips. The colors accentuated the pale pink shades of her dress and looked flattering with her natural coloring. Roberto Coin shared a photo of Selena from the award ceremony and stated the following.

A true beauty inside and out ✨ | @selenagomez wears #robertocoin to the Hollywood Beauty Awards.

Selena Gomez has a fabulous glam team as both her makeup artist Hung Vango and her hairstylist Marissa Marino both won awards. You may see a post that Hung shared along with his statement below.
