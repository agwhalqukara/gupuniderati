New Kids on the Block Reality Show: Woman Flashes Danny Wood in Clip

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Some rockers just don’t like the rocker lifestyle! In an exclusive new clip from Rock This Boat: New Kids on the Block, Danny Wood gets a crazy surprise from fan Amanda, known as “the wild child,” while innocently chatting with his manager.

Amanda, 38, gets frazzled when she sees the singer on deck, and quickly comes up with a way to grab his attention.

“I’m standing there getting so excited to see Danny and I get the idea that I have to do something even bigger, even better, even more shocking,” says the fan.

So, what does she do? Lifts up her top, of course! “Instantly he turns and goes to talk to his manager,” she says of his reaction. “And in my head I’m thinking, ‘I’m so excited! I’m getting my afterparty bracelet!'”
