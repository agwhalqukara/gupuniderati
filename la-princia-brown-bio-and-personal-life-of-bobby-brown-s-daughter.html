How Old is La’Princia Brown?

La’Princia Brown is 34 years old as of 2023. She was born on the 17th of September 1989 in East Weymouth, Massachusetts, United States of America. Her zodiac sign is Virgo, and she is a Christian. She is an American citizen of African-American ethnicity. La’Princia is one of Bobby Brown’s seven but five surviving children born to him by four different women.

She grew up in East Weymouth alongside her only biological sibling, a younger brother, Bobby Brown Jr., who sadly passed away in 2020 following a supposed alcohol, cocaine, and opioid fentanyl overdose. Who are La’Princia Brown’s Parents?

La’Princia Brown’s parents are Bobby Brown and Kim Ward, his one-time girlfriend. Bobby started his music career at the young age of 9 in 1978 and has remained active in the music scene ever since then.

He has released numerous hit singles, and some of his songs and albums that got commercial and critical success include Every Little Step, Something in Common, Don’t Be Cruel, and My Prerogative, among others. While Bobby Brown’s life, both professional and private, is an open book, there is little or no available information about La’Princia’s mother, Kim Ward, in the public space.
